# Arden Meridian

**Overhead insight. Ground-level decisions.**

Arden Meridian is a geospatial intelligence company focused on helping organizations make faster, clearer property and site decisions using satellite imagery, public data, structured analysis, and modern software.

We build tools and reports that turn complex location data into practical business insight.

---

## What We Do

Arden Meridian develops geospatial products for property intelligence, including:

* **Site analysis**
* **Commercial property ranking**
* **Solar-fit property reports**
* **Satellite imagery review**
* **Public-data enrichment**
* **Location-based decision support**

Our goal is simple: make geospatial intelligence easier to understand, easier to use, and easier to act on.

---

## Current Focus

We are currently focused on building property intelligence tools for businesses that need better visibility into commercial and industrial sites.

Our early work centers around solar prospecting reports that help solar companies identify promising commercial properties based on factors such as building size, roof potential, property type, and likely outreach priority.
